AI Ping Pong Game







Instructions

1. First keep your device screen straight

2. Move yourself approximately 3-4 feet away from your device

3. Move your right wrist up and down, and a red dot should appear on your wrist

4. Now press the play button and once again stand approximately 3-4 feet away from your device

5. The red line is the player's paddel, which will move along with your right wrist.
